About Us

Dee Global Collection is Port Harcourt's unique destination that brings together two essentials of modern life – exceptional dining and fashionable clothing. Located at No 24 Amaigbo Road, Ikokwu Mile 3, we pride ourselves on offering our community a comfortable space where you can enjoy a satisfying meal from our diverse menu while browsing through our curated collection of quality clothing for men, women, and children. Our commitment is simple: provide excellent service, delicious food, and stylish fashion that meets the needs of every member of your family.

What We Offer

Restaurant Services

Enjoy our extensive menu featuring local Nigerian favourites, continental dishes, small chops, and refreshing drinks. Perfect for breakfast meetings, lunch breaks, family dinners, or special celebrations. Dine-in, takeaway, and special event catering available.

Fashion Collection

Discover our carefully selected range of clothing for the whole family. From casual wear to corporate outfits, traditional attire to trendy pieces – we stock quality garments that suit every occasion and budget. New arrivals every week to keep you looking your best.
